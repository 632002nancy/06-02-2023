About Us

History:

Thrillophilia was founded in 2011 by Abhishek Daga, alumnus of the IIT BHU and Chitra Gurnani alumnus of the Indian School of Business and DA-IICT. Initially they used word of mouth and Digital marketing to popularize their company.
